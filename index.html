About

About WiE:

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering. At PESU South Campus IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is an Affinity Group that is dedicated to promoting women engineers in the field of science and technology. This is in association with the International WIE Organisation of IEEE which stands for inspiring and motivating young enthusiastic women around the world and to facilitate the recruitment and retention of women in technical disciplines globally. We envision a vibrant community which collectively makes use of their diverse talents to innovate for the benefit of humanity.

About WiT:

Women in Technology (WiT) is an annual event organised by PESU South Campus IEEE Student Branch to inspire, engage and advance women in engineering. The event provides professional women in technology, whether in industry, academia, or government a stage to tell their stories of success. This year, we are privileged to host six speakers who will be sharing their journeys on the platform. It aims to discuss how women can have a larger and crucial role in creating success stories in business and technology.
